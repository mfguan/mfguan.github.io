FEATURED RESEARCH

Understanding COVID-19 message fatigue

We have been bombarded with messages about COVID-19 since March 2020. As the pandemic drags on, information about COVID-19 is omnipresent and it is almost impossible to avoid it. How does message fatigue influence people's intention to engage in preventive behaviors? What types of information are people tired of hearing about? My collaborators and I conducted a study to answer these questions.

Tailoring health messages to enhance persuasion

Sometimes people are reluctant to engage in health behaviors, as the expected positive outcomes are often delayed. For instance, even when people are at risk of having obesity, they may consider physical exercise as not effective in losing weight in the short term. What strategy can we use to enhance health message effects? A short answer is message tailoring. Check out this article to learn more.
